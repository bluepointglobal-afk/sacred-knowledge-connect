Governance (draft)

The company currently manages sustainability topics across operations, finance, and HSE without a formally assigned executive owner. For audit-friendly governance, we recommend appointing a named ESG owner, defining cadence (monthly ops review + quarterly board review), and maintaining an evidence register.

Strategy (draft)

Sustainability priorities are driven primarily by client questionnaires and procurement requirements. A formal materiality assessment is not yet documented. A 90‑day sprint can establish a baseline emissions inventory and supplier policy, enabling credible disclosures.

Risk Management (draft)

Key risks include climate transition requirements from clients, supply chain compliance gaps, and incomplete safety leading indicators. We recommend a simple risk register with owners, controls, and quarterly review.
