Appendix: Methodology

A.1 Assessment Framework

This assessment uses the AFAQ ESG Assessment Framework v2.0, which evaluates organizations across four pillars:

  • Governance (25%): Board oversight, management structure, policies, ethics
  • Environmental (25%): Emissions, energy, water, waste, biodiversity
  • Social (25%): Workforce, health & safety, community, supply chain
  • Risk (25%): Climate risk, ESG integration, materiality, disclosure

A.2 Scoring Methodology

Each pillar is scored 0-100 based on:

  • Disclosure completeness (40%): Has the company provided the required information?
  • Evidence quality (30%): Is the information supported by documentation?
  • Performance (30%): How does the company perform vs. benchmarks?
