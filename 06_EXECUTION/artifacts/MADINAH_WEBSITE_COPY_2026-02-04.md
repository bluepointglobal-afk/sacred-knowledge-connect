## SECTION 1: VISION & MISSION

### Heading
**"Why Madinah?"**

### Body Copy
Madinah is the city of the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him), a place where Islamic civilization flourished and where students have studied the Quran and Islamic sciences for over 1,400 years.

The Madinah Project brings that tradition to students aged 12-16 in the USA, Canada, Europe, and UK. We offer:

- **Intensive Quranic study** with proper tajweed and memorization techniques
- **Islamic history & civilization** grounded in authentic sources
- **Character development** aligned with Islamic values
- **Peer community** of like-minded young Muslims building their faith together

## VISUAL DESIGN NOTES

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Gold (#D4AF37) - Islamic elegance, Madinah light
- **Secondary:** Deep green (#1B4332) - Islamic symbol, growth
- **Accent:** White/cream (#F5F5F0) - Purity, clarity
- **Text:** Dark gray (#2C3E50) - Readability

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Elegant serif (Georgia, Crimson Text) - scholarly feel
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif (Open Sans, Inter) - modern accessibility
- **Quotes/testimonials:** Italic serif - emphasis, authenticity

### Imagery
- Feature high-quality photos of Madinah landmarks (Quba, Kiblatain, Uhud, Prophet's Mosque surroundings)
- Student community photos (diverse, engaged, happy)
- Peaceful, golden-hour lighting throughout
- NO stock photos - use authentic, professional Madinah photography

### Layout
- Generous whitespace (Islamic design principle - clarity)
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Smooth scroll animations (subtle, respectful)
- Accessibility-first (high contrast, large text options)
