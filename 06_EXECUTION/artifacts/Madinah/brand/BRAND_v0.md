# Madinah Student Immersion — Brand v0

## Working name (v0)
**Nabawi Scholars Immersion**

Tagline options:
- *30–45 days in Madinah. Arabic. Qur’an. ‘Ilm.*
- *A turnkey Madinah immersion for students 12–17.*
- *From the classroom to the Rawdah — a structured ‘ilm journey.*

## Positioning
Premium, safe, structured, school-partnered student immersion in Madinah.

## Visual direction
- Islamic geometric patterning (subtle), not heavy ornamentation
- Calm, premium palette: deep emerald + night navy + sand + gold accents
- Typography: modern + readable (no cheesy “Arabic font” mimicry)

## Palette (suggested)
- Night Navy: #0B1020
- Deep Emerald: #0B3D2E
- Accent Gold: #C9A24E
- Sand: #E7D9B6
- Off-white: #F6F2E8

## Logo direction
- Minimal 8-point star / rosette + a path/road motif
- Optional secondary mark: stylized minaret silhouette (very subtle)

## Brand promise (1 sentence)
A safe, turnkey Madinah immersion that helps students build Arabic and Islamic studies foundations through structured study and daily circles at Al-Masjid an-Nabawi.
