## Design DNA (From References)

### Visual Style
- **Clean, modern Islamic aesthetic** (not traditional/heavy)
- **White/light backgrounds** with accent colors
- **Green accents** (Al Haramain uses teal/green, Jazari uses deep green)
- **Card-based layouts** for programs/features
- **Generous whitespace** - not cluttered
- **Professional photography** - students in natural settings
- **Minimalist navigation** - clear hierarchy

### Typography
- **Sans-serif primary** (modern, accessible)
- **Arabic calligraphy accents** (tasteful, not overdone)
- **Clear headings** with good contrast
- **Short paragraphs** - scannable content

### Color Palette (Nabawi Scholars)
```
Primary: Deep Emerald Green #0B3D2E (Madinah heritage)
Secondary: Warm Gold #C9A24E (excellence, aspiration)
Background: Off-white #F6F2E8 (warmth, approachable)
Text: Dark charcoal #1A1A1A
Accent: Light sage #E8F4F1 (cards, sections)
```
