Nabawi Scholars Immersion

A turnkey Madinah immersion for students ages 12–17: Arabic foundations, Islamic studies, and daily circles at Al‑Masjid an‑Nabawi, with accommodation, transport and catering provided.
Program formats
Option A
  • 30–45 day cohort
  • Best for depth + transformation
  • Target: 100+ students (partner networks)
Option B
  • 15 days × 2 per year
  • Lower friction for schools/parents
  • Builds recurring pipeline

  • Accommodation (supervised) + daily meals/catering
  • Transportation (airport + daily program movement)
  • Arabic instruction + Islamic studies schedule
  • Daily circle attendance at Al‑Masjid an‑Nabawi
  • End‑to‑end logistics support (including visa facilitation where applicable)
Partner responsibilities
  • Student selection + parent communications
  • Chaperone coordination (if required by partner policy)
