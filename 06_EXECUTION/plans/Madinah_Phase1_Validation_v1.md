## 4. Success Criteria for Validation Phase

### Phase 1 Validation Goals (8-12 Weeks)

#### Metric 1: Initial Interest / Response Rate
- **Target:** ‚â•40% of contacted schools respond (4+ schools out of 10)
- **Minimum:** 3 schools respond within 2 weeks
- **Tracking:** Email opens, replies, phone call requests

#### Metric 2: Discovery Calls Booked
- **Target:** ‚â•30% of contacted schools book a call (3+ schools)
- **Minimum:** 2 schools complete 20-min discovery calls
- **Tracking:** Calendar bookings, call completion rate

#### Metric 3: Serious Interest / Follow-Through
- **Target:** ‚â•20% of contacted schools request full program packet (2+ schools)
- **Minimum:** 1 school advances to proposal/contract stage
- **Tracking:** Document downloads, follow-up meetings scheduled

#### Metric 4: Student Interest Assessment
- **Target:** ‚â•50 students express interest across partner schools
- **Minimum:** 25 students from 2+ schools indicate willingness to participate
- **Tracking:** Interest forms, parent information sessions, preliminary registrations

#### Metric 5: Financial Feasibility
- **Target:** ‚â•60% of interested families can afford program with payment plans/scholarships
- **Minimum:** 15-20 students have demonstrated ability/commitment to pay
- **Tracking:** Financial aid applications, deposit payments, payment plan sign-ups

#### Metric 6: Partnership Quality
- **Target:** 1-2 schools commit to official partnership for pilot cohort
- **Minimum:** 1 school signs MOU or letter of intent
- **Tracking:** Signed agreements, commitment letters, deposit paid

#### Metric 7: Referral & Word-of-Mouth
- **Target:** Contacted schools refer 2+ additional schools/organizations
- **Minimum:** 1 organic referral or introduction from initial outreach
- **Tracking:** Inbound inquiries, "How did you hear about us?" responses
