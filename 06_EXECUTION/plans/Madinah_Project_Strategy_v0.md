## VISION

Create end-to-end facilitated Islamic education immersion programs in Madinah for kids aged 12-16 from Islamic schools in USA, Canada, Europe, and UK.

**Differentiation:** Full-service facilitation from visa to homecoming, institutional partnerships, quality assurance, and credible educational programming.

---

## CORE OFFERING

### Program Components
1. **Visa Processing & Facilitation**
   - Handle all documentation and coordination
   - Support families through the process
   - Leverage partnerships with Saudi cultural offices/consulates

2. **Logistics Coordination**
   - Flight arrangements (group bookings preferred)
   - Ground transportation in Saudi Arabia
   - Airport pickups and drop-offs

3. **Housing Arrangements**
   - Secure, supervised accommodations near Haram Nabawi
   - Age-appropriate facilities
   - 24/7 supervision and safety protocols

4. **Educational Programming**
   - Arabic language classes at Madinah Islamic University
   - Daily attendance at knowledge circles (halaqat) at Haram Nabawi
   - Structured Islamic studies curriculum
   - Cultural immersion activities

5. **Experiential Components**
   - Visits to historical Islamic sites
   - Community service opportunities
   - Peer learning and bonding activities
   - Character development programming

6. **Safety & Quality Assurance**
   - Background-checked staff and supervisors
   - 24/7 emergency support
   - Regular parent communication
   - Medical support and insurance coordination

## RISKS & MITIGATION

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|--------|---------------------|
| Visa processing delays/rejections | High | Start early, work with experts, have contingency timelines |
| Safety incidents | Critical | Rigorous staff vetting, 24/7 supervision, insurance, emergency protocols |
| Low enrollment (pilot) | Medium | Start with committed partner schools, offer early-bird incentives |
| Housing quality issues | Medium | Inspect facilities in advance, have backup options |
| Cultural/language barriers | Low | Hire bilingual staff, prep students with pre-program orientation |
| Regulatory changes (Saudi) | Medium | Stay informed, maintain flexibility, have local legal counsel |
| Reputational damage | High | Deliver exceptional quality, gather testimonials, transparent communication |
