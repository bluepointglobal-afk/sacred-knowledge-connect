# IDEA: Sacredchain

> Gate 0 Output | Created: 2025-01-20

---

## Problem Statement

**Muslims worldwide lack easy access to qualified scholars for sacred knowledge, while Western businesses struggle to find vetted Islamic experts.**

Current pain points:
- No unified platform connecting learners with renowned scholars globally
- Parents struggle to find trusted Quran/Islamic studies teachers for children
- Non-Muslims interested in Islamic history, arts, calligraphy have no dedicated learning platform
- Western businesses need Islamic consultants (halal certification, legal, education) but lack access
- Scholars have limited reach and no platform to both teach AND consult for businesses

---

## Solution

**Sacredchain** - A modern Preply-like platform with two verticals:

### 1. B2C: Learning Platform
- **Target 1:** Muslims seeking sacred knowledge (Quran, Hadith, Aqeeda, Spirituality)
- **Target 2:** Parents finding teachers for children
- **Target 3:** Knowledge seekers (non-Muslims) exploring Islamic history, arts, sciences, calligraphy

### 2. B2B: Sacredchain for Businesses
- Upwork-style consultancy for Islamic expertise
- **Services:** Halal certification, education consulting, legal advice (divorce, inheritance)
- **Target:** Western companies primarily
- Same scholars can teach AND bid on business assignments

---

## Platform Features

- Booking system (buy hours)
- Teacher profiles & discovery
- Session interaction tools
- Escrow system for confirming sessions
- Dual marketplace (teaching + consulting)
