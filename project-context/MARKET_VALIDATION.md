## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors

| Competitor | Model | Gap Sacredchain Fills |
|------------|-------|----------------------|
| **Qutor** | Online Quran tutoring | Quran-only, no broader Islamic sciences, no B2B |
| **Studio Arabiya** | Arabic & Quran lessons | Narrow focus, no scholar marketplace |
| **Bayyinah TV** | Pre-recorded courses | Not live, no 1:1 tutoring, no booking |
| **SeekersGuidance** | Islamic courses | Course-based, not tutoring marketplace |

### Adjacent Competitors

| Competitor | Model | Gap |
|------------|-------|-----|
| **Preply / iTalki** | Language tutoring marketplace | No Islamic vertical, no B2B consulting |
| **Tarteel / Quran.com** | Quran apps (AI-based) | Not live teaching, no human scholars |
| **Local Islamic centers** | In-person teaching | Limited reach, not scalable |
| **WhatsApp/Zoom tutoring** | Fragmented 1:1 | No marketplace, no discovery, no escrow |

### B2B Landscape

- Fragmented consulting firms
- No Upwork-style platform for Islamic expertise
- Companies rely on word-of-mouth or expensive specialized firms

**Key Insight:** No platform combines B2C tutoring + B2B consulting + global scholar marketplace.

| Advantage | Description | Defensibility |
|-----------|-------------|---------------|
| **Superior Tech/UX** | Modern, slick Preply-like experience vs fragmented WhatsApp/Zoom solutions | Medium - can be copied |
| **First Mover** | No dominant player in Islamic education marketplace | High - network effects compound |
| **Dual Marketplace** | B2C teaching + B2B consulting on same platform | High - unique positioning, scholars monetize both ways |

