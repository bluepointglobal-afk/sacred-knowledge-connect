# ICP — SacredChain (Islamic Learning Marketplace + Escrow)
*Generated: 2026-02-08*

## Ideal Customer Profile

**Primary Persona: Aisha**
- **Role:** Quran teacher (independent, imam's daughter)
- **Location:** London, UK
- **Students:** 5-15 students per week (online)
- **Income:** £500-2K/month from tutoring (supplemental)
- **Pain Point:** 
  - Finds students via WhatsApp/word-of-mouth (unreliable)
  - No platform trust/reputation system
  - Payment issues (students disappear mid-course, no escrow)
  - Isolated (no community with other teachers)
- **Goal:** Reliable student stream, safe payments, professional platform
- **Budget:** 10-20% commission on course fees (willing to pay for trust infrastructure)
- **Tech Adoption:** Moderate-high (uses Zoom, Google Workspace)

**Student Persona: Zain**
- **Age:** 25, working professional, London
- **Goal:** Learn Quran for personal development (2-3 hours/week)
- **Pain Point:** Concerned about teacher credentials/quality, doesn't want to commit to 6-month course, wants month-to-month flexibility
- **Budget:** £100-300/month for lessons
- **Trust Driver:** Reviews from other students, teacher certifications, money-back guarantee

**B2B Persona: Hassan**
- **Role:** Islamic education consultant / curriculum developer
- **Goal:** Create and sell pre-built Islamic learning bundles (complete Quran course, Islamic finance 101, etc.)
- **Budget:** 30-50% revenue share model acceptable
